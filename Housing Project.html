Housing Project

UoN Park Campus, Nottinghamshire, UK

Home Portfolio About

This housing project, situated on the University of Nottingham campus, provides accommodation for students, researchers, and faculty members.

The south blocks are designed at ground level to ensure accessibility for wheelchair users. On the north side, the buildings are elevated on stilts, allowing natural light to penetrate into all blocks. This raised design also creates versatile, open spaces beneath the buildings, which can be used for social activities. The development features a central courtyard, offering a semi-private outdoor space for relaxation and gathering.
