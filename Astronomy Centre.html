Edward Joseph Lowe Astronomy Centre

UoN Park Campus, Nottinghamshire, UK

Home Portfolio About

The Edward Joseph Lowe Astronomy Centre is an educational facility located on the University of Nottingham campus. It serves as a dedicated space for the astronomy department and an engaging visitor experience hub.

The building features an in-situ cast concrete structure, with the first floor set into the design. The external walls are supported by concrete fins on the ground floor, while sliding polycarbonate panels on the ground level provide dynamic shading elements. The sloped green roof enhances the building's sustainability but also offers a social space, allowing for a collaborative environment for students.
